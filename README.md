Balanced treatment of algebraic topology and category theory in parallel. Covers homotopy theory, fundamental groups, covering spaces, homology, cohomology, Poincaré duality, higher homotopy groups, fibrations, CW complexes, spectral sequences, sheaves and sheaf cohomology, de Rham cohomology, and the infinity-categorical perspective.

### 8. [Category Theory and Continuous Quality Improvement](./08-category-theory-cqi/category-theory-cqi-monograph.md)
**~12,000 words | March 2026 | Original research monograph**

Original work applying categorical formalism to healthcare quality improvement. Constructs the formal category **QSys**, develops the CAH-Qual subcategory for Critical Access Hospitals, formalizes the Donabedian functor, derives the PDSA monad, and develops enriched metric spaces for quality cost analysis.
